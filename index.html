May 2013 - Present

Responsible for recruitment, admission, advising, retention, and enrichment of all students in the undergraduate academic programs housed under The Department of Design at The Ohio State University. Serve as the primary point of contact for undergraduate programs with in the unit to all internal and external stakeholders including future students, families, employers, alumni, staff in collaborating units such as College and University level student services, and the general public. Coordinate, manage, consult on and advocate for internal and external processes, communications, and development as they pertain to the administration of the undergraduate curriculum at the department level.

"I help undergraduate design students design their Ohio State experience. It is my job to make the university small."

  • Academic Advising: Fosters student success through programs and strategies which support fulfillment of educational goals. Includes advising students on university policies, procedures, program and degree requirements and a range of other academic matters. Student orientation activities, materials preparation and distribution, logistics, teaching student survey courses and related actions are involved. Provides referrals to appropriate units for financial, mental health and other services. Includes collaboration with University Registrar, Student Financial Aid, University Bursar and other student support offices, and representing these services to students. Handles inquiries arriving via various channels, such as online, phone, mail, email and in-person.
  • Admissions and Recruitment: Responsible for student recruiting programs, pre-application and pre-admission, admissions applications reviews, systems data inputs and maintenance, admissions recommendations and decisions, and related processes as they pertain to unit’s competitive admissions processes. Includes interactions with students, families, external organizations and within the university for information exchange and issues resolution. Develop, implement and administer plans, programs and events for general and targeted student recruitment in support of organizational goals.
  • Academic and Curricular Program Services: Directly supports faculty and academic units in the development and delivery of educational offerings and programs offered by the university, ensuring support throughout the educational cycle from entry to graduation. Works with faculty to provide advice and coordination. Supports, coordinates and facilitates the implementation of various departmental curricula through service on standing and ad-hoc committees, as well as serving in various curricular job functions such as Transfer Credit Evaluator, Internship Coordinator, and Education Abroad Coordinator. It is not uncommon for me to attend meetings and update sessions on behalf of the Director of Undergraduate Studies, when they are unable to attend, as I work closely with them thought they are not my supervisor.
  • Registration and Records: Responsible for processes related to academic records, academic progress, and enrollment data. Process systemic enrollments requiring system overrides due to curriculum constraints and enrollment management policies. Forecasts and plans enrollment in collaboration with faculty for decisions on number of class sections to be offered; provides data services internally and externally in compliance with policies and regulations; supports services related to enrollment, advising and eligibility determinations. Liaison with offices such as University Registrar, roles involved in classroom allocation, capacity planning, electronic and other forms of document management, etc. Ensures that class schedules are up-to-date and entered in systems, enrollment data is reported and communicated as requested, and assessments are completed within the scope of job functions.
  • Marketing and Communication: Develops, implements, and oversees marketing and communications programs for the unit with long-and short-term strategies that support institutional priorities and business goals, while contributing to building the Ohio State brand. Manages the effective and efficient delivery of communications both internally and externally, via email, MS Teams, social media, and web platforms to further unit awareness, drive traffic to university and/or college/unit social media and/or web pages, and promote engagement towards other objectives. Collaborates across the enterprise on user experience, information architecture, and overall site content needs. Primary user and subject matter expert in web content management system within the unit, providing oversight and support while working closely with key stakeholders to ensure that web channel content is delivered with quality control, in compliance with policies and web standards and in support of business strategy and objectives.
